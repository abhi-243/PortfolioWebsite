About Me

Hi there, I'm Abhi Naglapura!

I'm a Mechatronic Engineering student at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS) with a passion for aerospace, rocketry, and robotics. I have a lot of hands-on experience with various controllers such as an ESP32 and Raspberry Pi, to name a few, and I’m currently working on building a full scale, fully functional R2-D2. I also enjoy looking into RF with my amateur radio license and exploring satellite frequencies. As a volunteer staff with the Australian Air Force Cadets, I also help mentor young cadets in leadership and aviation skills, and as a private tutor at Alchemy Tuition, I've helped students excel in subjects such as maths and science.

I’m very excited about the growing space industry in Australia, with an aim to work on rocket and aircraft propulsion systems. As such, I’m eager to bring my technical skills and passion for all things aeronatical to the aerospace field!
